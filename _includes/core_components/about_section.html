Elektra Cōmplex is a meeting of curious minds, eager ears and unassuming aesthetics; a group of dancers, lovers, and creatives, who took it upon themselves to create the experiences they have been longing for, with an open invitation at hand to all who want to join.

From art to fashion and parties, Elektra Cōmplex was created as a collective of sorts, embarking to offer a multitude of services that cater to the queer and the inquiring.
